FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

You've got questions. Here are some answers.

WILL THE EYE EXAM HURT?

Our procedures are non-invasive and will cause minimal to no pain. Some patients may notice a mild stinging with eye drops, but will only feel the sting for 5-10 seconds.

HOW LONG WILL THE EYE EXAM TAKE?

Generally, an eye exam will take between 15-20 or more minutes depending on the reason for visit. Please allow for 30-45 minutes including check in and check out.

WHAT HAPPENS TO THE INFORMATION YOU COLLECT?

Your privacy is important to us. All information is protected in secured and password protected facilities and kept for a minimum of 10 years. Data is backed up onto secure drives in case of computer failures. See our privacy policy here.

DO YOU TAKE INSURANCE?

Yes! See our list on the side to see if we bill your insurance directly. Have a question about your insurance? Give us a call and we make things clear for you.
