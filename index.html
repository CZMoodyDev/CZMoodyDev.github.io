KimonoPlay App

The Nikkei National Museum & Cultural Centre needed a mobile capable web application to serve as a companion app to their Kimono exhibit. The app is a dressup game where players choose the correct kimono based on their marital status, the season, and the formality of the occasion and learn about the art of choosing kimonos.

DesktopDungeonBot

I like to play games in my downtime and I thought it would be an interesting challenge to create a bot for Desktop Dungeons. It's a fun little strategy game. The bot traverses a grid and keeps track of tiles it can not step on, tiles occupied by enemies, and free tiles. There's also some more complicated game strategy involved to maximize profit that I won't go into here.

  • Python Application
  • Image Search and Manipulation
