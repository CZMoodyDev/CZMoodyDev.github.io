Comprehensive Eye Exams

Complete assessment of the eyes; it's more than just glasses

Pediatric (18 and under) and senior (65 and over) eye exams are covered by Alberta Health Care.

Medical Eye Exams

Medically necessary eye exams are covered by Alberta Health Services. Just inform us of the nature of your visit.

Be sure to bring your Alberta Health Care Insurance Number. Unsure if your visit will be covered? Check out Alberta Health Care's Vision Care Coverage.

Contact Lenses and Other Services

Contact lenses are a great way to see. Let us know if you are interested in a contact lens check. We also offer other services like occupational and drivers vision testing.

Additional fees may apply. Be sure to let us know while booking, during check-in, or with the doctor.
