Comprehensive Eye Exams

Complete assessment of the visual system including a refraction (your glasses prescription), and an eye health assessment. Children (18 and under) and seniors (65 and over) are covered by Alberta Health Insurance (Valid Alberta Health Number is required)

Medical Eye Exams

Health assessment of the ocular system from itchy and red eyes, to styes, headaches and everything in between. Be sure to bring your Alberta Health Number as this service is covered by Alberta Health. Unsure if your exam is covered? Give us a call or send us an email!

Contact Lens Fittings

Contact lenses are a great option for vision but require a more detailed visit. See Contact Lens Fitting for more information.

Refractive Surgery Referrals

Want to leave those glasses (and contacts) behind for good? Let us know and we can discuss your options for corrective surgery including LASIK, PRK, and RLE.

Occupational Services

Need an eye exam for your drivers license or report for a career? Let us know what you need at the time of your appointment.
