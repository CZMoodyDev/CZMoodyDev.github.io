Executive Board

Brad Lengsfeld

Vice Chair

Brad Lengsfeld has been involved with Blue Bronna since the early 2000s. He has spent several summers as a staff member and then continued to volunteer in various aspects. Brad has earned a Bachelor of Arts in outdoor education and leadership, through Prairie Bible college and then went on to earn a Masters of Arts in Leadership-Business through Trinity Western University. He has a passion for using the outdoors as a tool to teach people about God and community.

Chantelle Carlson

Marketing and PR

Chantelle Carlson has a background in Journalism and Public Relations and has worked as a journalist for both newspapers and magazines, as well as done marketing and PR for several para church organizations. Chantelle is married to Torie (a psychologist) and they have 4 energetic kids. They spend as much time as possible in the woods, on bikes, with fly rods, and on skis. Chantelle has been involved with Blue Bronna for the past decade, and has served on the board for five years.

Kent Lien

Secretary

Kent is a legitimate hiker, climber, skier, cyclist, hunter, and fisher and a wanna-be cowboy. He loves God’s Word and spending time in His Creation. He and his wife LeAnne live north of Cochrane with their three kids. Kent works in the oil and gas industry.

Ken Morris

Board Chair

Ken Morris grew up on a farm near Lampman, Saskatchewan. He went to his first camp at Kenosee Lake Bible Camp when he was 7 years old. He continued to go for the next 11 years and it was highlight of his life. He married the love of his life in June 1986 and has 2 daughters, a son, and a granddaughter. His kids spent a lot of weekends at camp and still take time to volunteer with him at camp when the opportunity arises. Camp has always been an important part of his life as it was life changing for him at the age of 7. He enjoys working with others to make sure kids have the opportunity to know who God is and that he cares for them. He has been serving on the Blue Bronna board for the past couple years and is honoured to work alongside the great people who care a lot for their faith and the work at Blue Bronna.

Graham Day

Treasurer

Graham was born and raised in Nigeria, Africa the son of career missionary parents. Upon returning to North America as a teenager, he finished high school and then attended Briercrest Bible College graduating in 1976. While at Briercrest he met the love of his life Diana, a farmer’s daughter and they were married in 1975. They have 3 grown children and 6 grandchildren who are all in Alberta. Graham and Diana have always been very involved in their local church serving in numerous capacities both teaching, leading worship and serving on governing boards. They currently live in Cochrane and are part of Bow Valley Baptist Church there. Diana was on staff in the financial office of the Baptist Seminary there for 10 years before retiring. Graham’s career has primarily been in the Insurance industry starting during their years in Saskatchewan where they raised their family and then continuing when they moved to Alberta in 2001. Graham was a claims manager for 15 years before moving to the brokerage side of the industry where he managed several Insurance brokerages till his retirement. Graham and Diana have served overseas numerous times on volunteer projects with missions. Graham was treasurer of Bow Valley Baptist church for many years and is currently the treasurer on the board of BBWC.
