This camp is designed to motivate this group of maturing pre-teens through trial and thrill. With activities planned for an active and developing age group, your children will experience adventure and challenge in a safe and engaging approach. Led by program facilitators that are certified in wilderness first aid, your child will be guided in developing leadership and outdoor skills.

You can expect your children to build relationships, and to start building competence in the outdoors and leadership through activities such as:

  • Canoeing
  • Rock Climbing
  • Rappelling
  • Campfire Cooking
  • Hiking/Scrambling
  • Horseback Riding (3 hours during the week)
  • Fishing
  • Field Games
  • Campfires
  • Bible Devotions

This camp is located at Willow Creek site. This remote site has enough amenities to be comfortable, however, we leave the luxuries behind. They will learn age relevant teaching from the bible around the campfire in the evenings and be challenged in God's Creation during the day. With staff to camper ratios of 6:1 our staff will guide your pre-teens through a challenging and thrilling journey.
