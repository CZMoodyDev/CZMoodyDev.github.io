4th Annual Josh Nichol Clinic

September 10-13, 2019

4th Annual Josh Nichol Clinic

The 3rd Annual Josh Nichol Clinic is being held September 10th to 13th at the Willow Creek location.

We have a VERY limited number of riding spots still available so book fast! This is your chance to build your horsemanship skills and spend time surrounded by the beauty of the mountains. As a rider, you will have a one hour session with Josh each day for three days, this is not an opportunity you want to miss out on!

To register call Kim Cooper at 403-498-7919

The Clinic is full for riders but we have lots of room for auditors.

Cost:

Riders - $800

Auditors - $20 per day or $50 for all four days (Bring your own chair)


Food - $25 per day

Accommodations (bunk) - $25 per night

Whatever kind of riding you do, whatever kind of horse you have, Josh Nichol's clear, gentle teaching will help you move towards a true relationship with your horse--one that enhances every aspect of horsemanship. Learn how Josh's goal of Softness, which he defines as "a state of flowing responsiveness free from any mental or physical resistance" can be achieved without force or gadgets.

To learn more about this great horseman, you can visit his website here.
