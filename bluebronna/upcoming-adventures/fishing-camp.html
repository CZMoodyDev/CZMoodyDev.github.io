Fly Fishing Camp

Join us for four full days of fellowship with God and God's Creation in Alberta's Rocky Mountains. Fish, pray, sing, and fellowship with men and young men that are seeking to become closer to Him. Experienced fishermen will be available to help all experience levels of fly fishermen.

World class fishing on the Old Man and Livingstone Rivers. Come by yourself or bring your teenage son along as well. Airport pickup is available.

Tents with bunks, and meals are provided. Bring your fishing gear, Bible and bed roll with pad and join us! Once you arrive at the scenic destination you will grow in your teamwork together as you help set up and keep camp running.

Expect to participate in:

  • Fishing Everyday - of course!
  • Starting Campfires
  • Gathering and Purifying Water
  • Chop Wood
  • Camp Chores - washing dishes and cooking
  • Helping Keep The Camp Running

Each day (weather permitting) you will explore the mountains and enjoy stunning views together. You will sing and enjoy evening campfire sessions encountering God. Did we mention how good food tastes cooked in cast iron over an open fire? This will be a trip you wonâ€™t forget.
