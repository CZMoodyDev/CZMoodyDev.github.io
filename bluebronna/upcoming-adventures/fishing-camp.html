This camp is open to men, women, and youth. Come by yourself or bring your son or daughter along as well. Airport pickup is available.

Attendees that arrive by plane in Calgary can contact us to carpool or share rides if space is available.

This camp is being held to our Coat Creek Site. This is located south on Hwy 40 just past the Livingstone Creek Campground - while going past our Willow Creek site.

Join us for four full days of fellowship with God and God's Creation in Alberta's Rocky Mountains. Fish, pray, sing, and fellowship with adults and youth that are seeking to become closer to Him. Experienced fishermen will be available to help all experience levels of fly fishermen.

Tents with bunks, and meals are provided. Lunch is not provided on the first day so grab a sandwich on your way in. Bring your fly-fishing gear, rain gear, Bible, and warm sleeping bag with insulated pad and join us! Once you arrive at the scenic destination you will grow in your teamwork together as you help set up and keep camp running.

Expect to participate in:

  • Fishing Everyday - of course!
  • Basic knot tying for fly fishing
  • Selecting flies
  • Sharing about fishes you caught and ones that got away
  • Camp Chores - starting campfires, washing dishes, and chopping wood
  • Helping Keep The Camp Running

Each day (weather permitting) you will explore the mountains and enjoy stunning views together. You will sing and enjoy evening campfire sessions encountering God. Did we mention how good food tastes cooked in cast iron over an open fire? This will be a trip you wonâ€™t forget.

We will fish together in small groups, so no one gets lost and for other safety reasons.
