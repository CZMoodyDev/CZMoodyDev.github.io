Need a break from your cell phone, the emails and the city? Want to experience for yourself what it’s like to camp at Blue Bronna before you tell your congregation about us? Come let us guide you in three days of fun activities in God’s creation where you can find rest and be refreshed. Led by program facilitators that are certified in wilderness first aid, you will participate in the following activities:

  • Hiking
  • Canoeing
  • Rock climbing
  • Rappelling
  • Campfire Cooking
  • Horseback Riding
  • Campfires

Enjoy the company of others who walk through similar joys and trials of ministry. This camp is located at our Coat Creek site. This remote site has enough amenities to be comfortable, however, we leave the luxuries behind. Accommodations include hunter style wall-tents with wood stoves and the good ol’ outhouse. Register today to get away from the noise and enjoy God’s beautiful creation.
