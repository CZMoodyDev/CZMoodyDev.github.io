August 1st to August 5th, 2023

Co-ed Ages 13 to 17

Dates:

Location: Coat Creek Wilderness Site

This camp is for anyone ages 13-17, whether you have been backpacking since you were walking or this will be the first time ever stepping outside. Experienced counselors will lead a group of about 8 into the back country near Coat Creek for 4 days and 3 nights of backpacking. Campers will learn more about who God is through counselor-led discussion in the evenings and by experiencing what He created as they hike.

As a base camp, you will camp in one spot, hike during the day and return to camp by supper. Evening chapels and games will happen at the base camp site.

Drop off and pick up by parents will happen at the Coat Creek site.

This remote site has enough amenities to be comfortable, however, we leave the luxuries behind. Campers will sleep in canvas wall tents, have food cooked over the fire, and will under a couple of large, tarped areas.

There is an outhouse but no showers.
