Looking for an adventure but not a horse person? How about some horsepower instead? Join us with your ATV (side-by-side/quad/etc) as we do some exploring of the backcountry for a few days! We supply all the food (which is super delicious), and sleeping accomodations, simply bring your own clothes/ATV/gear/personal affects and join us for an adventure!

Once you arrive at the scenic destination you will grow in your teamwork together as you help set up and keep camp running.

Expect to participate in:

  • Riding your ATVs Everyday - of course!
  • Starting Campfires
  • Gathering and Purifying Water
  • Setting Up Tarps for Sleeping
  • Setting Up The “Kitchen”
  • Chop Wood
  • Camp Chores - washing dishes and cooking

Each day (weather permitting) you will explore the mountains and enjoy stunning views together. You will sing and enjoy evening campfire sessions encountering God. Did we mention how good food tastes cooked in cast iron over an open fire? This will be a trip you won’t forget.

****Must bring your own ATV or rent one and bring your own fuel/insurance/helmet/riding gear
