Youth Adventure Camp: Ages 8 to 10

Youth Adventure Camp: Ages 8 to 12

At Willow Creek Resident Site

Our 8 to 10 year old camp is a great way to introduce your young children to the outdoors and God's Creation. With activities planned for a younger age group, your children will experience the wilderness in a safe yet engaging environment. Led by trained staff, your child is bound to be challenged and engaged for their adventure.

You can expect your children to gain confidence in the outdoors and build relationships through guided activities such as:

  • Canoeing
  • Rock Climbing
  • Hiking/Scrambling
  • Horseback Riding
  • Field Games
  • Campfires
  • Bible Devotions

This camp is located at our Willow Creek site in the foothills of the beautiful Livingstone Range. They will learn age relevant teaching from the Bible around the campfire in the evenings and be challenged in God's Creation during the day. With staff to camper ratios of 3:1 and a warm place to come home to at night, they can experience the thrill of the land with comfort and confidence.

Facilities include cabins, washhouse with showers, toilets and sinks, food cooked in our kitchen and a couple of tarped areas where we eat outside.

Start Time: Sunday at 4pm

End Time: Friday at 2pm
