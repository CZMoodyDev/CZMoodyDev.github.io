Single Parent Family Camp

Single Parent Family Camp is geared for single parents to come and enjoy time as a family and to connect with other single parents. There will be activities during the day, campfires and snack in the evenings and session time with a speaker with topics geared to single parents. During these sessions, your children will be cared for by our staff in a short session of their own as well as activities.

Location: 12km down Hwy 532 South of Longview

This camp is located at our Willow Creek site where you will stay in cabins, wall tents, or you can bring your own trailer. This site has all the amenities that a family could need including indoor plumbing at our washhouse, showers and covered eating and campfire areas. Come enjoy the company of your family, build relationships with other families, encounter God through dynamic teaching and worship times and build memories that will last.

Cost: $200 per family of 5 for the weekend. $25 per child after that. Kids under 5 are free. This cost includes all accommodations, food, and activities. There is financial sponsorship if needed.

Activities:

  • Horseback riding
  • Real rock climbing and rappelling
  • Canoeing and Kayaking
  • Archery
  • Hiking in the mountains
  • Hatchet throwing
  • Games
  • Chapel

Arrival Time: At 7:00pm on Friday

Departure Time: By 2:00pm on Sunday
