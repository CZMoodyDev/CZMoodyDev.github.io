Teen Base Camp will push you in your spiritual walk, your outdoor skills and your leadership ability. Our engaging and challenging week will drive you to realize your potential. We will guide you in a week of thrilling adventure, challenging conversation and incredible victory. Led by program facilitators that are certified in wilderness first aid, you will be supported in growing your leadership ability and refining your wilderness savvy.

You can expect to build relationships, to gain confidence in wilderness settings and develop leadership through activities such as:

  • Overnight Out-Trips
  • Canoeing
  • Rock Climbing
  • Rappelling
  • Campfire Cooking
  • Hiking/Scrambling
  • Horseback Riding
  • Field Games
  • Fishing *you will need to bring your own fishing gear
  • Campfires

This camp is located at our Willow Creek site. This remote site has enough amenities to be comfortable, however, we leave the luxuries behind. Campers sleep in canvas tents with a wood stove for heat and most of the food is cooked over the fire. You will learn age relevant teaching from the Bible and be challenged in God's Creation. With staff to camper ratios of 6:1 our staff will guide you through a challenging and thrilling journey.
