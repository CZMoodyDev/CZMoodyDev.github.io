July 30th to July 5th, 2024

Co-Ed Ages 13 to 17

This new program focuses on outdoor living skills: fires, shelter building, tools, first aid, as well as individual skills and knowledge: edible plants, snares, solar stills, and whittling.

Campers will learn more about who God is through counselor-led discussion in the evenings and by experiencing what He created as they live outdoors.

Evening chapels and games will also happen at the camp site.

Drop off and pick up by parents will happen at the Coat Creek site.

This remote site has enough amenities to be comfortable, however, we leave the luxuries behind. Campers will sleep in canvas wall tents, have food cooked over the fire, and will under a couple of large, tarped areas.

There is an outhouse but no showers.
