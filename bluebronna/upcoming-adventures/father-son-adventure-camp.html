Blue Bronna is all about creating an environment where dads and sons spend time together and are encouraged in their relationship with each other and with God.

This is a great opportunity to re-connect or connect deeper with your son and spend time “Away from the Noise” to focus on the important things in life. Join us and invest in a fun weekend, and receive an abundant return on that investment.

However. Before you race to grab your hiking gear. Keep in mind this is not deluxe housing…it’s rustic, but you and your son will love it. This camp is located at our Willow Creek site. This remote site has enough amenities to be comfortable, however, we leave the luxuries behind. Campers sleep in canvas tents with a wood stove for heat and most of the food is cooked over the fire. We’ll provide the nutrients, nesting place and neutron-packed program for a weekend you won’t forget.

You can expect to participate in activities such as:

  • Climbing and Rapelling – To build self-confidence.
  • Fishing in the creek beside camp – Quiet moments to talk and experience the thrill of the catch.
  • Exploration on the hiking trails – For the sheer enjoyment of the outdoors.
  • Horseback Riding – Enjoying the sites and sounds while riding on these magnificent animals. Our day trips will take you either along the Livingstone Range or the Continental Divide.
  • Sitting around the Campfire – Hear from God through His word and through a speaker.
  • Canoeing
  • Field Games
  • Fishing* you will need to bring your own equipment
  • Campfires

Any combination of men and guys are welcome; it’s not just for fathers and sons. Sons bring your dad, grandfather, or uncle and spend some quality time together!

Open to ages 13 to 88!

All meals, snacks, and accommodations are included in the camp fee. The food sure tastes cooked in cast iron over an open fire.
