Family Camp - August 2nd to 5th

Family Camp

Looking for a family friendly vacation? This is it! To help you connect to your family and to other families as you enjoy the wilderness. Our goal is to help you grow in your walk with God and your experience with the wilderness as well as grow your relationship with your family and other families. Letâ€™s not forget that we have so much fun. Led by program facilitators that are certified in wilderness first aid, your family will participate in the following activities:

  • Hiking
  • Overnight Out-Trips
  • Canoeing
  • Rock Climbing
  • Rappelling
  • Campfire Cooking
  • Horseback Riding
  • Field Games
  • Campfires
  • Horse Feeding Chores
  • Dish Chores
  • Biblical Teaching Sessions

Families will participate in activities together. As well, our program facilitators love to take your kids on an adventure away from camp to give the adults in the family quiet time alone. This camp is located at our Willow Creek site where you will stay in cabins or wall tents. This site has all the amenities that a family could need including indoor plumbing at our washhouse, showers and covered eating and campfire areas. Come enjoy the company of your family, build relationships with other families, encounter God through dynamic teaching and worship times and build memories that will last.

Start Time: 7pm Friday

End Time: 2pm Monday

Cost: $130 per adult, $80 for youth ages 11-17, $50 for kids ages 6-10, 5 and under free, Family max of $600.
