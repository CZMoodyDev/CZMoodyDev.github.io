Looking for a quick get away with the family that's affordable and stress free? Join us Family Day Long Weekend in Alberta, for a relaxing, and fun few days with the whole family at the willow creek site! With cozy cabins for you to enjoy, we plan to have bonfires, skating, ice fishing, hiking, and maybe even sleigh rides! We looking forward to seeing you there!

Activities are based on weather and availability and are subject to change without notice.
