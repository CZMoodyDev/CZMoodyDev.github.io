Looking for a quick get away with your family that's affordable and stress free? Join us for a great weekend for a relaxing, and fun few days with the whole family at the Willow Creek site! Winter is a beautiful time to enjoy the mountains but it usually comes with a steep price tag.

At Blue Bronna Wilderness Camp, we love bringing families together to enjoy the wilderness in a family friendly, Christian based environment. With cozy cabins for you to enjoy, we plan to have bonfires, skating, ice fishing, hiking, snowshoeing, cross country skiing and sledding! You are free to bring your snowmobile as well as kids love being pulled behind one on a toboggan.

We are looking forward to seeing you there!

Space is limited due to available cabin space. Accommodations for 5 families for the weekend and more if people share (relatives) or a small family is willing to stay in a trailer. The maximum space is for 25 people

Please bring your own skates and any ice fishing equipment you have as well as lots of warm layers!

Activities are based on weather and availability and are subject to change without notice.

Cost: $60 person, Kids 5 and under are free.

Feb 16-19 - $250 family max for the weekend for up to 5 people, $25 per person after that.

If there is enough interest, we will run one or two in March.
