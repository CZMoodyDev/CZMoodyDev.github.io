Winter Family Days

February 15th - 18th, 2019

Looking for a quick get away with the family that's affordable and stress free? Join us Family Day Long Weekend in Alberta, for a relaxing, and fun few days with the whole family at the willow creek site! Winter is a beautiful time to enjoy the mountains but usually comes with steep price tag, at Blue Bronna Wilderness Camp, we love bringing families together to enjoy the wilderness in a family friendly, Christian based environment. With cozy cabins for you to enjoy, we plan to have bonfires, skating, ice fishing, hiking, and maybe even sleigh rides! We looking forward to seeing you there!

Space is limited due to available cabin space.

Please bring your own skates and any ice fishing equipment you have as well as lots of warm layers!

Activities are based on weather and availability and are subject to change without notice.

Arrival: 7pm Friday

Departure: 11am Monday
