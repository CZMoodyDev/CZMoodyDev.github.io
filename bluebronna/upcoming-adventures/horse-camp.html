Willow Creek Girls Horse Camp

Ages 13 to 17

Dates: June 30th to July 5th, 2024 | Ages 13 to 15

Dates: June 14th to July 19th, 2024 | Ages 15 to 17

8 Participants and 3 Staff

Your daughter can come to camp and sharpen her horsemanship skills! Our three instructors will match their teaching with your child’s level of expertise. They will explore different areas of horsemanship including:

  • Western
  • Bareback

Bring your own horse or use ours. This clinic style camp is great if your child wants to do nothing but horse related activities. This camp is focused both on arena riding and trail rides through the foothills. Let us show your youth how her relationship with their horse reflects a relationship with Jesus. She will enjoy great food, great company, great teaching and fun evening campfire singing/worship and biblical teaching times.

This camp is located at our Willow Creek site where your daughter will stay in a cabin or a wall tent. This site has all the amenities that they could need including indoor plumbing at our washhouse, showers and covered eating and campfire areas.

Start Time: 4pm Sunday

End Time: 2pm on the last day of camp

Cost: $550

Co-ed Teen Horse Camp - Coat Creek

Dates: July 21st to July 25th, 2024

Co-Ed Ages 13 - 17

8 Participants and 3 Staff

You can come to camp and sharpen your horsemanship skills! Our instructors will match their teaching with your child’s level of expertise.

Bring your own horse or use ours - they will need to be shod to ride here.

This camp is great if your youth wants to do nothing but ride horse. They will learn some horsemanship but will spend most of their time on trail rides through the foothills and mountain areas around camp. Let us show your youth how his/her relationship with their horse reflects a relationship with Jesus. They will enjoy great food, great company, great teaching and fun evening campfire singing/worship and biblical teaching times.

This remote site has enough amenities to be comfortable, however, we leave the luxuries behind. Campers will sleep in canvas wall tents, have food cooked over the fire, and will under a couple of large, tarped areas.

There is an outhouse but no showers.

Start Time: 4pm Sunday

End Time: 2pm Friday

Cost: $550
