Ladies Horse Camp

July 16th to 19th, 2020

"The land is breathtakingly beautiful and vast. You will never see such stars anywhere else!"

Did you miss out on camp as a kid? Love horses, but are a bit rusty? Want a fun weekend getaway with other horse crazy ladies? Well, here's a weekend for you! No Horse? No problem! We have horses available for use at no extra cost.

Just a weekend of fun together riding in the arena, spend the day out on the trail and up on the mountains and in the evening have camp fires. Want to go for a hike up a mountain instead? No problem- take a day off the riding and hike!!

Camp out in your trailer or make yourself comfy in one of our cabins.Â Camping is included in your fees. There are NO hook ups, but quiet generators are welcome.

Horse accommodations: Reserve a stall *limited space available*, use the group turn out pen (individual pens MAY be available), bring panels, tie to your trailer, or use your trailer. You are responsible for bringing hay/feed, bucket for water, tack, etc. for your horse.

This weekend is geared towards those who are looking for a weekend away from everyday life and need one full of horses and other horse crazy gals. On this laid back weekend, take time to experience God and His creation, moments to hear His voice, make new friends, learn new things, brush up on skills, relax and have a good time.
