August 6th to August 11th, 2023

Teen Climbing Camp

This camp is being run for those teens that love to rock climb.

Campers will learn to climb as well as learn the climbing set up, learn to belay and safety practices under the direction of trained staff.

They will climb at various climbing spots in the area, stay in tents, and cook their food at the campsite.

Campers will learn more about who God is through counselor-led discussion in the evening and by experiencing what He created as they live outdoors.

Evening chapels and games will also happen at the camp site.

Drop off and pick up by parents will happen at a campsite near Canmore, AB

Start Time: 4:00pm

End Time: 2:00pm

Cost: $490.00
