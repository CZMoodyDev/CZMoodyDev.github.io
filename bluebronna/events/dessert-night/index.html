Music and Dessert Night in High River

We are excited for an event this Spring in High River.

We are having a Music and Dessert night at the High River Baptist Church on Sunday April 23rd from 7:00pm to 8:30pm.

Music will be by the Nipawin Bible Institute music group.

There will be plenty of great desserts.

There is no cost but a free will offering will be taken.
