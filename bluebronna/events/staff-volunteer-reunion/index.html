Staff and Volunteer Reunion

Dates: June 16th to June 18th, 2023

For all past staff and volunteers

This is a great chance to get reacquainted, to see what is going on at the camp, to hear of the future plans and to spend time with family and friends.

Activities will be open on Saturday at no cost.

Accommodations and food provided.

You can stay in a cabin, bring a tent or trailer, or just come for the day.
