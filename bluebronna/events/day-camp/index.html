2022 Day Camp Registration

Blue Bronna Wilderness Camp offers kids, youth, adult and family camps.

We are located 30 minutes from Longview close to Indian Graves Campground.

CAMP ACTIVITES

These include: horseback riding, rock climbing, hiking, archery, canoeing and kayaking Climbing requires a 10-15 minute drive and a hike up a hill - a bit strenuous. We have riding helmets, riding boots, lifejackets, and paddles. There will also be a morning and evening Chapel time with singing, puppets and a talk on the Bible. It is fun and not pushy.

MEALS

Lunch and Supper is provided in the fee.

$25 Reservation Fee. This will show up at the end to pay with a credit card. The rest to be paid at camp.
