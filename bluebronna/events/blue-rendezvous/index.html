31st Annual Blue Rendezvous Fundraiser

*****Location! We are very excited to be hosting this event again. It will take place at the Centre Street Church - Bearspaw Campus - North Calgary*****

The 31st Annual Blue Rendezvous Fundraiser is being held on January (27th) as one of the key fundraising efforts that support our programming.

Join us for an evening for fun and fundraising! This dinner and pie auction is definitely an event you don't want to miss!

Register

Have we mentioned pie?

That's right, after dinner you get to bid on which dessert or pie you get to savour for the evening! Each table pools together their money and bids on desserts!

We strive to keep our camper fees down so that the camps we provide remain affordable to those who otherwise could not come. We typically see 40% of our costs covered by the camper fees we charge and the other 60% is made up from donations to the organization.

Everyone is welcome!
