Junior Staff Training

This program is an intentional 3-week training program for those youth who are 15 or 16 at the time of the camp. There will be intentional training in God’s Word, camp activities, and camper issues, as well as fun times and a 4-5 day back pack trip in the mountains. There is also opportunity for those who are 14 to come and help for a week as well as those who are 17 to come for up to 7 weeks in the summer.
