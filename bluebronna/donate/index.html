Donate

At Blue Bronna we fully depend on God's provision to meet our operational budget. We do not have denominational or corporate support. Camper fees cover about one third of the cost to run a camp, therefore, we rely on donations to cover the shortfall. A large majority of these donations are received through our two main fundraising events: ride-a-thon in September, and Blue Rendezvous in January. We are so thankful for the continuous and generous support we receive year after year at these events. This ministry could not continue to thrive without your support.

Blue Bronna is looking for monthly partners to help us meet our fixed costs. These costs consist of operational and salary costs. Our goal is to raise $10, 000 in monthly reoccurring donations. While this appears like a large number, every little bit helps. We ask you to prayerfully consider your vital partnership with us through a regular monthly donation. Our goal of $10,000 can be met by donating using the following options:

Monthly

Online through Paypal

You can set up an account or use an existing paypal account to make monthly donations using many different payment methods including all major credit cards. Funds will then be transferred into Blue Bronna’s account automatically each month.

Through your Bank

You can also donate monthly through your bank. Print off this form to fill out and return to the camp. Funds will then be transferred into Blue Bronna’s account automatically each month.

One-time

Online through ATBCares

You can donate through ATBCares using any major credit card. Please include your address and email so that we can send a thank you note. Automatic tax receipts are issued to donors through the Canadian Online Giving Foundation. The receipt will not have Blue Bronna Wilderness Camp on it.

One-time Donation (ATBCares)
E-Transfer

You can also send an e-transfer to office@bluebronna.org. Please include your address and email so that we can send you a donation receipt or a thank you note.
