Family Rentals Registration

The information below may be out of date. Please email office@bluebronna.org for full details before registering or review the following document

Due to COVID-19, summer camps are cancelled. We will be open for family rentals during the following dates.

Willow Creek will be open June 19th with Coat Creek being open June 26th. Both will be available until August 30th.

Social Distancing

Activities: One family at each activity at a time; families will need to drive to the canoeing and climbing locations while our staff take a separate vehicle. All items will be sanitized after use.

Accommodations: One family per cabin

Food Services at the Dining Hall: We eat outside under a tarped area -- rain or shine. One family per large picnic table.

How will sharing of the bathroom work?: One family at a time; sanitized after use. There are also 3 outhouses on site.

Coat Creek: You will be the only family on site.

Willow Creek: There will be two to three other families on site.

Accommodations: Cabins at Willow Creek and large canvas wall tents with wood stoves and mesh bunks at Coat Creek.

Activities at both sites will have a cost. These include:

  • Horseback riding (except Sundays)
  • Climbing/rappelling (except Sundays)
  • Canoeing

You can fish at both sites as well as go hiking. You can also do archery at our Willow Creek site.

Guides will be available for Horseback riding, climbing/rappelling and hiking if you like.

A quick lesson can be given at archery.

Costs

Lodging: Cabin at Willow or Tent at Coat Rental - $50 per night

Food: Breakfast $4, Lunch $5, Supper $6 per person

  • Option 1 - You can prepare your own food

    Option 2 - Pay for food at our café at Willow

    Option 3 - You can choose which meals you want at Willow

At our Coat Creek Wilderness Site, you will need to bring and prepare your own food.

Activities:

  • Horses and Climbing/rappelling - $20 person each activity

    Horses – we have helmets and boots if needed.

  • Canoeing and Archery $7.50 person

    Canoeing – we have lifejackets and paddles.

Family Max Cost: To be determined based on the size of your family.

Reservation Fee: 50$ - This will show up at the end to pay with a credit card.

24 hour time slot between groups.

Additional Policy

NO ALCOHOL NOR RECREATIONAL MARIJUANA IS ALLOWED ON EITHER SITE. THOSE NOT ABIDING BY THIS WILL BE ASKED TO LEAVE.
