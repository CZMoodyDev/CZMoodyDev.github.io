Custom Static Websites

I can build you a custom static website to showcase your talent, spread the word, or be your living resume. I can host your website so it is accessible to all and maintain it so that it is always synced to your needs.

Here's how it works:

  1. You give me your idea and any resources you have available (the colors you want, images you want displayed, etc)
  2. I send you an invoice for the amount the project will cost
  3. You send payment or deposit
  4. I build the website and deploy it to a test website for any critique or approval
  5. When we're both happy, I'll push the website live
